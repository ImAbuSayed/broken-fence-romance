[Acoustic Romantic Americana Intro]
[Soft fingerpicked guitar and warm atmospheric reverb]

[Hook]
I can feel the heat in the shadows of the room
When you look at me like a late-night moon
We've been talking close with our glasses half dry
Now there's something burning in the deep of your eye

[Verse 1]
We walked the long road to the edge of the town
Where the old broken fence is tumbling down
You lean on the wood with a smile so slow
Drawing me in where the headlights glow

[Pre-Chorus]
Every single breath is pulling us near
Whispering the things that I want you to hear
I can taste the honey on the edge of your lip
Just one touch and I'm ready to slip

[Chorus]
Oh the midnight is heavy and the fire is sweet
In this broken fence romance where our two worlds meet
Wrap your arms around me let the quiet turn loud
We're the only two people in a neon crowd

[Post-Chorus]

[Verse 2]

Fading to black as we're slipping away
